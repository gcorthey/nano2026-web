Why Nano2026 is a valuable opportunity

The XXIV Meeting on Surfaces and Nanostructured Materials is the annual meeting of the nanotechnology community in Argentina. Since its first edition in 2001, organized at the CNEA Bariloche Atomic Center, it has become a leading venue for presenting and discussing advances in nanoscience and nanotechnology.

The 2026 edition will be held in person at the National University of San Martin and will be organized by INS-EByN-UNSAM, in collaboration with UBA, CNEA, FAN, UNLP and CONICET. This edition will place special emphasis on strengthening interaction between the scientific community and the productive sector.

The event will bring together researchers, students, professionals and technology leaders in an ideal environment to build visibility, strategic connections and meaningful conversations about applications, industrial challenges and development opportunities.
